Why I started Nourish the Needy

The goal is simple: raise money, buy quality ingredients, and make hot meals for people staying in local shelters. I document every cook day with photos, receipts, and a short blog update so you can follow the journey.

What your donation funds

  • Fresh ingredients for meals
  • Cooking supplies and packaging
  • Transportation to the shelter

How often we cook

  • Monthly cook days
  • Target: 100 to 200 meals per visit
  • Photos and receipts after each visit
